About TACHYON

SEO, Web Crawling and Web scraping

Are you wondering how to improve your blog page rankings on Google? Do you want to acquire the most sought after skills in digital marketing? Then look no further!

IEEE IAS DAIICT Student Branch Chapter proudly presents Tachyon; a highly comprehensive, applied and certified Bootcamp on Search Engine Optimization(SEO) on 15th and 16th September, 2018. With its wide range of uses, SEO is a thriving skill to acquire in this era of digital transformation.

Bring on your enthusiasm and motivation and we will equip you with the basics. The 2-day bootcamp will walk you through a range of technical aspects associated with SEO and a hands-on training to learn Web Crawling, Web Scraping and SEO algorithms.

Register now! We have a limited number of seats. Hurry up!
